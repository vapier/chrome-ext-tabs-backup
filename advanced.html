Tabs Backup & Restore - Advanced Rate on the Chrome Store
With Advanced Restore you can restore a custom selection of your backups!
  • Click on a Backup entry to expand it, and view all the windows that were backed up.
  • Click on a Window entry to view all the tabs it contained.
  • Select the checkboxes to make your selection.
  • Press the "Restore Selected" button when you are ready to restore.
